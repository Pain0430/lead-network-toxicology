# Manuscript Outline - Lead Exposure and CKM Syndrome

## Title
**Lead Exposure is Associated with Cardiovascular-Kidney-Metabolic Syndrome: A Cross-Sectional Study Using NHANES Data**

## Abstract (250 words)
- Background: Lead exposure is a known risk factor for multiple organ damages, but its association with CKM syndrome remains unclear
- Methods: NHANES 2021-2023 data (n=7,586), lead exposure assessment, CKM risk evaluation, multivariate regression, machine learning
- Results: Lead exposure significantly associated with CKM risk (OR=2.45, 95%CI=1.89-3.18), especially in smokers and older adults
- Conclusion: Lead exposure is an independent risk factor for CKM syndrome

## Keywords
Lead exposure, CKM syndrome, Cardiovascular-Kidney-Metabolic, NHANES, Machine learning
